About this Event

The biggest Afrobeats celebration Lagos has seen this year. Lagos Soundwave Festival brings together the hottest artists, DJs, and creatives for a night of culture, music, and pure energy at Landmark Beach.

Expect live performances, immersive art installations, food vendors, and an atmosphere that captures everything that makes Lagos the cultural capital of Africa. This is not just a concert -- it is an experience.

Doors open at 7PM. The main stage kicks off at 8PM sharp. VIP tables available -- contact the organiser directly for bookings. Dress code is smart casual. No outside drinks. All ages welcome before 10PM, 18+ after.

This event is fully licensed and security approved. Emergency services will be on standby. Parking is available at the Landmark complex.
